About

I'm a data scientist and full-stack developer who got tired of watching East African businesses manage inventory over WhatsApp and Excel. So I built TaalumaERP — offline-first inventory and business management software for businesses operating across East Africa and Europe.

My background spans data science, machine learning, NLP, and full-stack development. I trained at Constructor Academy and Code Institute, and I've worked across PHP, Python, JavaScript, and Django — but my focus now is building products that solve real problems for underserved markets.

The AI side of what I build isn't decoration. TaalumaERP has RFM analysis, churn prediction, ABC stock classification, anomaly detection, and a semantic similarity chatbot — built by a data scientist, not bolted on as a feature.

When I'm not building, I'm thinking about the gap between how software is built in Europe and what actually works in East Africa. That gap is the opportunity.
